# Executive Summary

Acme Holdings reported consolidated revenue of $4.2B for Q4 2025, representing a 12% year-over-year increase driven by growth in the insurance and real estate divisions. Operating margin improved to 18.3% from 16.1% in the prior year period.

| Metric | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue | $4.2B | $3.75B |
| Operating Margin | 18.3% | 16.1% |

Total operating expenses for Q4 2025 were $3.43B, an increase of 9% from the prior year. The primary drivers were higher compensation costs and increased technology spending.

# Insurance Division Performance

The insurance division generated $1.8B in premium income with a combined ratio of 94.2%. Policy retention rates remained strong at 91%, and new policy issuance increased 15% year-over-year.

# Real Estate Portfolio

The real estate division reported $980M in revenue from 147 commercial properties. Occupancy rates averaged 96.3% across the portfolio. Three new acquisitions closed during the quarter.
